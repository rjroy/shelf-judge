Interaction notes
Desktop: Hover highlights card with border + shadow lift. Clicking card or its button submits the pick immediately — no confirm step. Next pair loads inline without navigation.
Mobile: Tap-to-select state shown above — tapping selects (shows checkmark), which could then auto-submit after a brief delay, or require a second tap. Either works; the brief-delay approach reduces accidental picks on mobile.
No skip: Per spec, every comparison produces a result. "End session" is the exit if the user can't choose.
Scores shown: Both axis fitness and tournament rank are visible. If either is missing (unranked/no axes), that slot shows "not yet ranked" in muted italic.
Progress bar: Decorative only — sessions have no fixed length. It shows comparisons-this-session as a reference point, not progress toward a goal.
